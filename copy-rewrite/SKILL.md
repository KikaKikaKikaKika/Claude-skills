## Style Guide Rules to Apply

### Voice
- Professional, conversational, practical, genuine — part of the team, helpful, clear
- Use "we" for the company, "you" for the user
- Gender-neutral: use "they/their"
- Avoid inserting company name unnecessarily

### Core Principles
- Every word has a purpose — cut filler, redundancy, and padding
- Under 30 words per sentence
- Short paragraphs (max 5–6 sentences), most important info first
- No jargon — use plain, familiar language
- ~Sixth-grade reading level; avoid long complicated words
- No excessive adjectives
- Present tense (not future)
- Active voice (not passive, unless avoiding self-reference or the object matters more)
- Positive framing (tell users what to do, not what they can't)
- Inclusive language: "they", "people", "underrepresented groups", "people with disabilities"

### US Spelling
- "z" spellings: realize, organize, customize
- "ense": license, offense
- "og": dialog, catalog
- No double-l: modeling, paneled
- color, center, while, fulfill

### Punctuation
- Spell out "and" — never "&"
- Oxford comma in lists of 3+
- No exclamation marks unless truly warranted (max one per page)
- Reword questions as affirmative statements where possible
- No periods in: buttons, headings, notifications, toasts, tooltips, nav items, placeholders
- Use periods in: full sentences, body text, help text, descriptions
- Introduce bullet lists with a colon
- Use "…" (ellipsis character), not three periods
- Em dash with spaces: word — word
- En dash for ranges: 50–100
- No semicolons if avoidable
- No ampersands

### Capitalization
- Sentence case for headings and buttons (first word + proper nouns only)
- Lowercase after slashes: ZIP/postal
- List items start with a capital letter

### Articles
- Omit articles (a, an, the) in headings and UI labels
- Use articles normally in body/full sentences

### Numbers & Abbreviations
- Zero–nine: spell out (unless technical)
- 10+: numerals
- Spell out full words: "application" not "app"
- Spell out acronyms first time
- Don't start sentences with numerals
